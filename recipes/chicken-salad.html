Chicken Salad

Description:

This is a recipe for Chicken Salad.
Chicken Salad is a Southern favorite and is great for hosting events.
There are many variations of Chicken Salad, but this is my favorite way to make it.

Chicken Salad isn't actually a salad in the normal sense of the word.
It is great on crackers, on bread a sandwich, or even rolled up in Romaine Lettuce as a light snack.
Follow along, and let's make some delicious Chicken Salad.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of boneless / skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 pack of chicken breast tenderloins
  • 1 cup Mayonnaise
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 Yellow or Vidalia Onion
  • 2 tbsp yellow mustard

Steps:

  1. On the stove, place a large pot of water to boil.
  2. Once water has come to a boil, place chicken thighs, and breast tenderloins in water. Turn down to simmer.
  3. Place another medium pot of water on stove to boil.
  4. Once second pot of water is boiling, carefully place eggs in water, let boil for 20 minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes, move pot of eggs to sink, and run cold water in pot until all hot water is gone.
  6. Remove shell from Eggs. Pat dry eggs with paper towel.
  7. Remove chicken from stove, and drain water. Use hand chopper, and chop chicken to small shreds.
  8. Place eggs in chicken, and use hand chopper to chop eggs, mixing with chicken.
  9. Add Mayonnaise, Mustard, Salt, Pepper, and Sugar to mixture.
  10. Chop Onion, and put in mixture.
  11. Mix all ingredients together.
  12. Let cool to room temperature, or place in refrigerator to cool. Enjoy!
