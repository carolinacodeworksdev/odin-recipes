Turkey

Description:

Turkey is a delicious entree that can be made anytime, but is most popular during holiday meals.
This recipes gives you a simplified way to cook a delicious, beautiful Turkey.
It is easy to mess up a Turkey, so you must pay attention to your bird!.

There are several different ways to cook a Turkey, but this is a very simple recipe.
Some people prefer the Turkey stuffed, some do not. This recipe will not include stuffing.
All of the ingredients can easily be found, anytime of the year, so no need to stock up during holidays.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Turkey (The size can vary, but we will assume it is about a 20 pound turkey).
  • 1 Turkey Bag. Usually found on the baking aisle of your supermarket.
  • 1 Stick of Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Orange
  • 1 Potato (White Russet is fine)
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat Oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Remove Turkey from wrapper. Remove any giblets inside Turkey, and throw away (or keep for stuffing).
  3. Rinse Turkey off, and make sure legs are secured together.
  4. Place Turkey inside Turkey bag, and set inside pan large enough to hold Turkey.
  5. Place 1 stick of butter inside Turkey.
  6. Slice Orange, Potato, and Carrot into Turkey bag.
  7. Sprinkle Salt, and Black Pepper over Turkey inside the bag, then secure the bag with twist tie.
  8. Place Turkey inside oven, and let cook for about 4 to 4 1/2 hours.
  9. Open the bag, and let Turkey cook for the last 3o minutes of cook time, until skin is golden brown.
  10. Remove Turkey from oven, remove from bag, and serve. Enjoy!
