Spaghetti

Description:

This is a recipe for a basic dish of Spaghetti, and Italian, and American favorite.
This dish is simple to make, and will produce a delicious meal that will satisfy your whole family.
The entire cook time for Spaghetti is about 45 minutes at most, (unless you prefer a homemade version).

Spaghetti can be made by teenagers, and adults alike, and can be kept in the refrigerator for leftovers also.
With just a few simple ingredients, your entire family can enjoy a delicious Italian feast.
Follow along, and let's get started with the recipe for Spaghetti.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can / jar of Spaghetti Sauce (Personal Preference)
  • 1 box (1 lb.) of Spaghetti noodles (May substitute with gluten free noodles)
  • 2 pounds 80%/20% Ground Beef or Ground Chuck
  • 1 tbsp crushed red peppers
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar

Steps:

  1. On the stove, place a medium pot of water to boil.
  2. Heat a pan, and place ground beef in it to brown.
  3. Once Water has come to a boil, turn down to simmer, and place noodles in water. Stir occasionally.
  4. Once ground beef is browned, remove from heat, and drain grease. Return to heat, and add spaghetti sauce.
  5. Add Salt, Pepper, Crushed Red Pepper, and Sugar to the Spaghetti Sauce, and Ground Beef, and stir well.
  6. Let Sauce heat to a simmer, and reduce heat to low.
  7. When Spaghetti noodles are soft, drain water from pot, and return to stove.
  8. Pour Sauce mixture over noodles, and stir together, (Some may prefer to keep them in separate pots)
  9. Put Spaghetti, and Meat Sauce on plate and serve hot. Enjoy!
